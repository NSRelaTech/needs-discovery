## 🔑 Key Need Identified

### **Community Event Discovery & Sharing**
**Reported by:** Stari Grad resident

**The Gap:**
- Lack of easily accessible community event calendars
- No centralized way for residents to post upcoming gatherings
- Difficult to discover what's happening in the neighborhood

**Desired Solution:**
- A streamlined tool for posting and viewing neighborhood events
- Focus on fostering neighbor-to-neighbor connections
- Easy accessibility for all residents

---

## 💡 Implications for NSRT Principles

This need aligns well with NSRT's core principles:

✅ **Neighbor-to-Neighbor:** Events posted by residents, for residents (not top-down from government)  
✅ **Village-Scale:** Focused on neighborhood/block level gatherings  
✅ **Simple Tool:** A calendar/event board is a discrete, focused solution  
✅ **Relational Tech:** Directly facilitates in-person connections and community building
